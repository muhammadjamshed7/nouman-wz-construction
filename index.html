Our Construction Services

Comprehensive solutions from foundation to finishing

Turnkey Construction

Complete end-to-end home construction services for plots of all sizes (3, 5, 10 marla, 1 kanal). From architectural design to final touches, we handle everything.

  • Customized designs to match your vision
  • Quality materials selection included
  • Regular progress updates

Grey Structure

Expert structural work including foundation, columns, beams, bricks/block work, roofing, and plastering. Ideal for clients who want to handle finishing themselves.

  • Earthquake-resistant construction
  • Certified materials for structural integrity
  • Engineering-grade precision

Finishing Work

Complete interior and exterior finishing including tiling, flooring, painting, woodwork, and all final touches that transform a structure into a home.

  • Premium finishing materials
  • Modern aesthetic designs
  • Attention to every detail

Material Supply

Reliable supply of quality construction materials including bricks, sand, cement, electrical installations, and plumbing fixtures at competitive prices.

  • Certified quality materials
  • Timely delivery
  • Transparent pricing

Renovation & Upgrades

Transform your existing space with our renovation services - from structural changes to interior upgrades, kitchen remodels, and bathroom renovations.

  • Minimal disruption
  • Modern design integration
  • Property value enhancement

Consultation & Planning

Professional guidance on your construction project including feasibility assessment, budget planning, design advice, and permit acquisition.

  • Expert recommendations
  • Cost optimization strategies
  • Regulatory compliance
